​Introduction​

The blockchain industry and the infrastructure of the Internet of Value are being built rapidly around the globe, and to many the atmosphere is eerily similar to the building of the Internet in the late ‘90s, with pioneers and dreamers coming together to build a new future. Tomo network and Tomocoin can be a leading part of this phenomenon through seamlessly merging social and consumer applications used by millions of mainstream users with a native crypto-currency with unique blockchain architecture, allowing for fast, frictionless payment and a secure, decentralized, and trustless store of value.

Wallet

In our opinion, a secure and user-friendly coin wallet that gives financial control back to the end-users is one of the most important parts of the infrastructure of the “Internet of Value”. It will bridge the gap between a cryptocurrency enthusiast and a mainstream user – and possibly increase the adoption rate of cryptocurrency by 10-fold if done right. We want to invest in building such a wallet, firstly to host Tomocoin and Ethereum and later multiple other cryptographic tokens and currencies.
