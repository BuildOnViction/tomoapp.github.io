​Introduction​

We are building a new social network from ground up with a built-in cryptocurrency. The core values are:
- Meaningful connection: topics-based social graph. Help solving other's problems; create and publish useful contents and receive reward from the network.
- Built-in micro payment with a store of value characteristics: unique architecture allowing for an easy monetization with micropayment in publishing, gaming, crowdfunding etc. but also a secure store of value of coin on a public blockchain.
- First rate usability using technology such as mobile first, AI, AR, location and camera based services.
- User privacy: no tracking, no data mining and surveillance; allowing for anonymous identity and content creation.

Wallet

In our opinion, a secure and user-friendly coin wallet that gives financial control back to the end-users is one of the most important parts of the infrastructure of the “Internet of Value”. It will bridge the gap between a cryptocurrency enthusiast and a mainstream user – and possibly increase the adoption rate of cryptocurrency by 10-fold if done right. We want to invest in building such a wallet, firstly to host Tomocoin and Ethereum and later multiple other cryptographic tokens and currencies.
To safely store Tomocoin, Ethereum, and other coins and tokens

Debit Card

Connect your Tomo account wallet to your Tomo debit card to spend your coins as fiat currency anywhere in the world. Fees will be minimal and as the card uses Visa infrastructure you can use it almost anywhere in the world.
